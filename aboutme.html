The ART of designing experiences.

I'm a Junior UX/UI Designer from Mexico — I create the interaction between humans and products, in order to make products functional, usable, accessible, and enjoyable to use for everyone.

Design is for me an opportunity to always look for ways to improve the customer's experience. As a designer I understand that the perfect design doesn't only contains visual aesthetics, for me it's also all about championing the user.

I have learned to apply human-centered and user-centered design methods as an emphasis to create value, as well as to always have empathy with the user. This means that the user needs are always my central focus on every project. I translate user needs into concepts, user flows, wireframes, mockups, prototypes and high-fidelity tests.
